LAN File Share Application - User Guide
=========================================

1. STARTING THE APP
-------------------
Double-click on the "run_app.bat" file (create this handy script if you like) or open a terminal in this folder and type:
    python app.py

Keep the terminal window OPEN. If you close it, the server stops.

2. CONNECTING YOUR PHONE
------------------------
Option A: Scan the QR Code
    1. Look at the "QR Code" box on your computer screen (browser).
    2. Open your phone's camera.
    3. Scan the code to instantly open the file share page.

Option B: Manual Connection
    1. Connect your phone to the SAME Wi-Fi network as this PC.
    2. Type the address shown on your PC screen (e.g., http://192.168.1.5:5000) into your phone's browser.

3. SHARING FILES
----------------
To Upload (Send to PC):
    1. Click "Choose File" on your phone or other device.
    2. Select the photo or document.
    3. Click "Upload". It will be saved to the "uploads" folder on your PC.

To Download (Get from PC):
    1. Put files into the "uploads" folder on your PC.
    2. Refresh the page on your phone.
    3. Click "Download" next to the file name.

4. TROUBLESHOOTING
------------------
- "Site can't be reached" / "Connection timed out":
    Check your Windows Firewall. You must allow "Python" to communicate on Private networks.
- QR Code doesn't work:
    Make sure both devices are on the exact same Wi-Fi. If using a hotspot, ensure data is allowed.
